Name: Calorie & Macro Tracker (Python)
Author: David Cao
Development Start Date: 11/12/20
Development Finish Date: 11/30/20


Features:
-User can set a daily calorie & macro target/goal.
-User can enter calories in/burned. App calculates net calories & compares it to user's target.
-User can enter macro information and compare it with target goal.
-User can view their weekly progress & see it on a graph.
